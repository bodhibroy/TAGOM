Welcome to Vocal Matcher

In this game, we are going to play one note to you. The objective of the player is to sing back the note in the closest possible voice.

There is a progress bar with an arrow sign next to it indicating your progress. If you're singing in a lower pitch, the arrow will point upwards, indicating that you should sing in a higher pitch. In case you are singing in a higher pitch, the arrow will point downwards, indicating that you should sing in a higher pitch.
